These 10 experts were selected because they:

- **Demonstrate expertise** in content strategy, automation tools, and scaling content production

Their combined perspectives represent diverse approaches to AI-powered B2B SaaS marketing, from technical SEO optimization to go-to-market strategy and international scaling.

## Research Methodology

**Verification Process:**

1. **LinkedIn Profiles:** Verified active profiles reflecting current professional roles and areas of expertise
2. **YouTube Channels:** Confirmed public channels with recent publications on relevant topics
3. **Content Collection:** Systematically collected 5 recent LinkedIn posts and 3 video transcripts per expert
4. **Structured Organization:** Organized all content by expert in markdown format for easy access and analysis
5. **Quality Standards:** Ensured all collected content demonstrates substantive expertise in AI-powered SEO and B2B SaaS content production

All expert profiles, links, and content sources are publicly available and independently verifiable through LinkedIn, YouTube, and other public platforms.
