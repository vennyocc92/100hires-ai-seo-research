## Red Flags & Authenticity Checks

### Signs of Genuine Experts:
- ✅ Consistent content publishing (weekly or more)
- ✅ Long history in the field (3+ years minimum)
- ✅ Published at least one comprehensive guide
- ✅ Speaking appearances at industry conferences
- ✅ Authentic engagement in comments/responses
- ✅ Transparent about tools and affiliations
- ✅ Regular YouTube content or blog updates
- ✅ LinkedIn profile with 500+ connections

### Warning Signs to Avoid:
- ❌ Only sells courses, no free content
- ❌ New accounts with sudden popularity
- ❌ No publishing history before 2020
- ❌ Heavy affiliate linking without disclosure
- ❌ No engagement or response to followers
- ❌ Unverified claims about credentials
- ❌ Inconsistent or outdated content
