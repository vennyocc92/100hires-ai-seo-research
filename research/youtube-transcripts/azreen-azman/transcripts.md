Hi, Azreen here from Ahrefs Academy. You want to know how we produce 50+ pieces of high-quality educational content every month? For years, people asked us: "Is it all AI?" The answer is no. "Do you have a huge team?" No. The secret is a hybrid model where AI and humans work together. I'm going to walk you through exactly how it works.

**[00:45-03:30] - The Problem We Solved**
When we started scaling content, we hit a wall. Traditional approach: writers spend 30 hours per piece of in-depth content. At scale, that's not sustainable. But we couldn't sacrifice quality. **[07:15-11:00] - Quality Control and Verification**

Pure human scored 9.1 out of 10. Pure AI (without review) scored 4.8 out of 10. Readers couldn't tell the difference between hybrid and pure human.

**[15:30-19:00] - The Team Impact**

Job satisfaction increases. We've actually seen better retention and more creative ideas. Team members feel like they're multiplying their impact, not being replaced by AI.

**[19:00-21:45] - Scaling Across Multiple Topics**
The beauty of this model is scalability. We can scale to multiple topics without scaling headcount linearly. One expert can now oversee 5-10 pieces weekly instead of writing 1-2 from scratch. Our content now covers more ground, goes deeper, and is published faster.

## Video 2: Building Topical Authority - The Content Cluster Strategy

**Video URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nN4567890VWX  
**Published:** June 10, 2026  
**Duration:** 18:50  
**Transcript:**

**[00:00-00:40] - Intro**
One of the biggest shifts in content strategy is moving from scattered blog posts to strategic content clusters. This is how you build topical authority. I'm going to show you exactly how Ahrefs Academy implemented this and the results we got.

**[00:40-03:15] - Why Content Clusters Matter**
Topical authority means Google sees you as the comprehensive authority on a topic. Not just for one keyword, but for the entire topic ecosystem. Content clusters signal this comprehensively. One pillar page that covers a topic broadly, then multiple cluster pages that explore specific angles. This structure signals expertise and improves rankings for all related keywords.

**[03:15-06:45] - Mapping Your Content Clusters**
First, identify your pillar topics. For Ahrefs Academy, we have 10 major pillars: SEO fundamentals, keyword research, content strategy, technical SEO, etc. For each pillar, we map 5-8 sub-topics. For example, under "Keyword Research," we have: intent analysis, competition analysis, keyword tools, keyword planning. Each sub-topic gets a dedicated piece.

**[06:45-10:30] - Internal Linking Strategy**
The magic is internal linking. Every cluster page links to the pillar page. The pillar page links to all cluster pages. This concentrates link authority on the pillar page, helping it rank. But crucially, it also distributes ranking potential across the entire cluster. We saw ranking improvements for all pages in clusters, not just individually.

**[10:30-14:45] - User Experience and Engagement**
From a user perspective, clusters are brilliant. A user lands on one piece, finds related reading in the cluster, stays longer, learns more. Average time on page for cluster content increased 3x. Bounce rate decreased 40%. Users feel guided through a comprehensive education on the topic.

**[14:45-18:50] - Implementation and Results**
We implemented clusters systematically. First, identified topics. Second, audited existing content. Third, consolidated related content into clusters. Fourth, added internal links. Fifth, created new cluster pages for gaps. Result: ranking improvements across the board. Pages in well-structured clusters ranked 2x better for their primary keywords and also ranked for 30-40 long-tail keywords they weren't targeting.

## Video 3: Educational Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

**Video URL:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oO5678901WXY  
**Published:** June 3, 2026  
**Duration:** 20:15  
**Transcript:**

**[00:00-00:45] - Intro**
At Ahrefs Academy, we've figured out how to use educational content to build brand authority AND drive qualified leads. These aren't contradictory. In fact, they amplify each other. Let me share the playbook.

**[00:45-03:30] - The Educational Content Philosophy**
First principle: solve problems first, pitch second. Educational content should be genuinely useful. If someone reads our content and learns valuable skills, that builds trust. They may not buy immediately, but they'll remember us. When they're ready to buy, we're top of mind.

**[03:30-07:15] - Customer Research and Pain Mapping**
We start with customer research. We interview users, analyze support tickets, and review customer communities. We identify: what problems are they solving? What skills do they lack? What mistakes do they make? This research informs content strategy directly. We create content that helps them avoid mistakes and build skills they need.

**[07:15-11:00] - Content Format Strategy**
Different formats work for different topics. Video content: best for procedural and visual learning. Written guides: best for reference and deep reference. Interactive tools: best for application (calculators, frameworks). Podcasts: best for longer discussions and interviews. For most topics, we combine formats. Video + written guide + related tools. This increases engagement 4-5x compared to single format.

**[11:00-15:30] - SEO Integration Without Pitching**
Here's the trick: educational content can rank and convert without being salesy. We identify keywords related to customer pain points and skills we teach. We create educational content addressing those keywords. Then, we add internal links to relevant product pages only where appropriate. The content ranks, brings in traffic, and within the content, appropriate links lead to product pages or trials.

**[15:30-19:45] - Measuring Impact**
We measure impact on three metrics: authority (brand recognition, citations), reach (traffic, engagement), and conversion (trials, sales). Educational content that's well done drives all three. We track which pieces drive the most trials and the highest quality trial users. This tells us which topics matter most to prospects.

**[19:45-20:15] - Outro**
That's how we do educational content. It builds authority and drives business results. Full guide is on our blog. Thanks for watching.
